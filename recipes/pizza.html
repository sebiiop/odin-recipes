Pizza Magherita

Ingredients

Procuce

    5 Basil, large fresh

    2 Garlic cloves, fresh
    1 cup San marzano tomatoes, canned

Baking and Spices

    1/2 tsp Active dry yeast
    2 1/2 cups All-purpose flour, unbleached
    1/4 tsp Black pepper, freshly ground
    3/4 tsp Diamond crystal kosher salt
    1 tsp Granulated sugar
    2 large pinches Kosher salt
    1 Red pepper flakes, dried
    1 Semolina and all-purpose flour

Oils & Vinegars

    1 1/3 tbsp Olive oil, extra virgin

Dairy

    7 oz Mozzarella cheese, fresh
    2 tbsp Parmigiano-reggiano cheese

Others

    7 ounces (105°F to 115°F) warm water
